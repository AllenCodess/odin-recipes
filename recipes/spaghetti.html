Description

Tender, marinated steak grilled over an open flame, thinly sliced, and tucked into warm corn tortillas.

Topped with fresh cilantro, diced onions, and a squeeze of lime, these tacos bring bold, smoky flavors with every bite.

Served with your choice of salsa for the perfect balance of heat and zest.

Ingrediants

  • Grade A Wagyu Steak
  • Fresh Pico De Gallio
  • Fresh Guacomole
  • Mozzerla Cheese

Steps to make the tacos

  • Cook the steak to your liking
  • Mix all the ingreidiants for the Pico De Gallio
  • MIx all the ingrediants for the guacomole
  • Put the tortillas on the pan for about two minutes or until golden brown. Butter them up
